About KwicksparK Tutoring

A student‑run tutoring initiative focused on making math and coding less scary and more fun.

KwicksparK offers friendly, patient, and structured 1‑on‑1 sessions. We break problems into simple steps, explain the “why” behind each concept, and give students space to ask questions without feeling rushed or judged. Whether it’s algebra homework, coding projects, or exam prep, we focus on building real understanding—not just memorizing formulas.

What we tutor

Core math and beginner‑friendly coding support.

Middle school math

Algebra & Geometry

Python programming

Variables, loops, conditionals, functions, and small projects to build confidence.
